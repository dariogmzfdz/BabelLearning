filter-scale works!

In the 1:18 scale, one centimeter of the miniature represents 18 centimeters of the real model, or what is the same; 18 centimeters of the real model are represented in 1 centimeter of the miniature. To know the measurements of a 1:18 scale model, we will have to divide the measurements of the real model by 18, and so on for each of the different scales.
